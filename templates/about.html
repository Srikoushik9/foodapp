About our website and our Group

Food and beverage Company where you can order and track your food

At University of Central Missouri, we are passionate about food and technology. Our goal is to make it easy and convenient for users to order their favorite meals from local restaurants, while providing powerful tools for restaurants to manage their businesses. We leverage the latest technologies and AWS services to create a secure and reliable platform that can scale to meet the needs of growing businesses. Our use of AWS Codepipeline and EC2 allows us to provide fast and responsive service, while our use of RDS and S3 ensure reliable and scalable data storage. Thank you for choosing The Heartland Chow. We look forward to serving you!
